About Liverpool FC

Liverpool Football Club (Liverpool or The Reds) is a professional football club based in Liverpool, England. Founded in 1892, Liverpool joined the Football League the following year and has been playing their home matches at Anfield Stadium ever since.

For much of Liverpool's history, its home colours have been all red.
